In order to display an experience inside of a web page or application, embed code must be used. Experiences also offer the option to generate a landing page to share the experience. Lesson experiences also have an option to download a SCORM package.

Follow these steps to generate the embed code for an experience.

  1. Open the Interactivity module.
  2. In the left navigation, click Experiences > All experiences.
  3. Click an experience Title or click the action menu (...) associated with the experience and click View.
  4. Click Embed.
  5. (Optional) Click Get URL to get a URL to the experience that can be shared with others.
  6. (Optional) Click Preview to open a preview of the experience in another browser tab.
  7. Select the embed code to use.
    • Standard (iframe) - Recommended for most cases. Places the player inside an <iframe> tag. If player dimensions are specified, the iframe will be sized to the player dimensions. If no player dimensions are specified, the iframe can be placed inside of a <div> tag and the responsive nature of the player will resize the player to occupy the entire space of the <div>.
    • Advanced (JavaScript) - Allows the player to exist in the the HTML page directly, not in an iframe. This makes it easier for developers to access the player and associated properties and events. By using the advanced embed code, you lose the ease of use of the player as a single, contained unit in social media applications and sharing.
  8. Click Done.

Generating a landing page

Experiences can be shared with viewers either by embedding the experience in a web page or by sharing a landing page. A landing page is a single page that a viewer can access at a static URL. Follow these steps to generate a landing page.

  1. In the left navigation, click Experiences > All experiences.
  2. Click an experience Title or click the action menu (...) associated with the experience and click View.
  3. Click Embed.
  4. Click the Landing Page tab.
  5. (Optional) Click Get URL to get a URL to the experience that can be shared with others.
  6. (Optional) Click Preview to open a preview of the experience in another browser tab.
  7. Copy the URL to the landing page.
  8. Click Done.

Downloading a SCORM package

Lesson Experiences offer the ability to download a ZIP package that can be imported into a SCORM compliant LMS. Follow these steps to download a SCORM file.

  1. In the left navigation, click Experiences > All experiences.
  2. Click on a Lesson experience Title or click the action menu (...) associated with a Lesson experience and click View.
  3. Click Embed.
  4. Click the SCORM tab.
  5. Click Download to download the SCORM package.
  6. Click Done.
