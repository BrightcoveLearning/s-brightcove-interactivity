An Experience is a video with other elements (like synced slides or an e-commerce cart) outside the video player that dynamically interact with video player. Experiences allow you to dynamically communicate between a video and the web page itself, opening the door to a new generation of lean-forward interactive realities for your viewers.

Creating a new experience

Follow these steps to create a new experience.

  1. Login to Video Cloud Studio.
  2. Open the Interactivity module.
  3. In the left navigation, click Experiences > All experiences.
  4. Click New Experience.
  5. Enter an Experience Name.
  6. Choose an experience type.
  7. Click Next .
  8. Select one or more projects to add to the experience.
  9. Click Next .
  10. Confirm the experience settings and then click Create.

For information on creating the different types of experiences, see the related topic:

  • Branching
  • Lesson
  • Slide Sync
  • Off-Video Chapters Menu

Editing an experience

Follow these steps to edit an experience.

  1. Open the Interactivity module.
  2. In the left navigation, click Experiences > All experiences.
  3. Click on an experience Title or click the action menu (...) associated with the experience and click View.

Configuring experience settings

The experience settings can be used to change the experience title, tags and some other settings. Follow these steps to configure the experience settings.

  1. Open the Interactivity module.
  2. In the left navigation, click Experiences > All experiences.
  3. Click an experience Title or click the action menu (...) associated with the template and click View.
  4. Click Settings.
  5. Edit the settings as needed. When done click Save.

Experience settings

  • Auto Progress - Will play the next video in the playlist after the previous project finishes
  • Auto Play - Will auto-play on landing page load
  • Interactive Experience CSS (URL) - URL to a custom CSS file used to style the experience
  • Tags - Tags for the experience, tags facilitate searching experiences

Branding settings

Experiences can be shared with viewers either by embedding the project in a web page or by sharing a landing page. A landing page is a single page that a viewer can access at a static URL. The branding settings can be used to style the appearance of the landing page.

  • Landing Page Header HTML - HTML that will be displayed on the landing page header
  • Landing Page Footer HTML - HTML that will be displayed on the landing page footer
  • Background Image - Background image for the landing page

Adding projects

Follow these steps to add additional projects to an experience.

  1. In the left navigation, click Experiences > All experiences.
  2. Click a template Title or click the action menu (...) associated with the experience and click View.
  3. Click Add project.
  4. Select additional projects to add.
  5. Click Apply.

Changing the play order

Follow these steps to change the play order of projects in an experience.

  1. In the left navigation, click Experiences > All experiences.
  2. Click a template Title or click the action menu (...) associated with the experience and click View.
  3. Click Change play order.
  4. Click on a project and drag it to its new location in the play order.
  5. Click Save.

Moving an experience to a folder

Folders can be created and used to organize experiences. You must have created folders before you can move an experience to a folder. Follow these steps to move an experience to a folder.

  1. Open the Interactivity module.
  2. In the left navigation, click Experiences > All experiences.
  3. Click the action menu (...) associated with the experience and click Move to Folder.
For more information on folders, see Working with Folders.

Duplicating an experience

Duplicating an experience is a great way to save time when creating new experiences that are similar to ones already created. Simply add/remove projects and edit the experience settings as needed.

Follow these steps to duplicate an experience.

  1. Open the Interactivity module.
  2. In the left navigation, click Experiences > All experiences.
  3. Click the action menu (...) associated with the experience and click Duplicate.
  4. Click Duplicate.

This will create an exact clone of the experience including all projects, tags, and custom CSS, titled "{experience_name} - Copy".

Deleting experiences

To delete an experience, find the experience on the experience list, click the action menu (...) associated with the experience and then click Delete.
