The Sign In widget (commonly referred to as a "lead wall", "turnstile" or "gate") is a flexible, powerful tool that lets you track known viewers. It is used to generate leads, to have learners and trainees sign in, or to add a gate to a video. Viewers will only have to sign in once, and we'll automatically remember them every time they watch a Brightcove Interactivity video on the same device.

Adding a sign in annotation

Follow these steps to add a sign in annotation to a project:

  1. Login to Brightcove Interactivity Studio.
  2. In the left navigation, click Manage.
  3. Click on a project to open it.
  4. Click the widget icon in the bottom toolbar.
  5. Click the Sign In widget.
  6. Click ADD. The configuration dialog will open.
  7. Enter the text for the Title and Sub-Title.
  8. Use the slider to determine which fields to display. It is also possible to add an additional, Custom Field.
    • First Name
    • Last Name
    • User Id
    • Email
    • Optional Custom Field
  9. Click on the color options to select colors.
    • Background Color
    • Text Color
    • Button Background Color
    • Button Text Color
  10. Configure the options. 
    • Skip - Hide or show the skip button on the sign in form
    • Gated - When set to Active, video will pauses and wait and the viewer can't skip ahead using the seek bar
    • Privacy Policy - Hide or show the privacy policy URL
    • Privacy Policy URL - Enter the URL to the privacy policy
  11. Click Save. 

Viewing Sign in results

The Signin Widget Summary report can be used to review the number of logins across the group. The Signin Widget Detail report will display a list of all logins. To access the Signin Widget Detail report, follow these steps:

  1. Login to Brightcove Interactivity Studio.
  2. In the left navigation, click Core Reports.
  3. Select a Date Range for the report.
  4. At the top of the page use the report selector to select the Signin Widget Detail report.
  5. Click RUN.

The report will display a summary of logins.
